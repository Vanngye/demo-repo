About Green Banks

Located on Anjanapura 80 Feet Rd, Green Banks is a serene gated community in Anjanapura 6th Block, Lal Bahadur Shastri Nagar, Bengaluru. Our mission is to create a space where residents can enjoy fresh air, minimal pollution, and a healthy, green environment. At Green Banks, greenery is not just a feature—it’s a way of life.

Why Choose Green Banks?

  • Ample green cover with over 70% of the area dedicated to parks and plantations.
  • Zero-tolerance approach to air pollution with strict green policies.
  • Walkways and cycling tracks surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Rainwater harvesting and eco-friendly waste management systems.

Amenities

While we prioritize greenery, we understand the need for basic amenities. Green Banks offers:

  • Open-air yoga spaces and meditation zones.
  • Children’s play areas designed for safety and fun.
  • Community gardens for residents to grow fresh produce.
  • 24/7 security with eco-friendly patrol vehicles.
